HOW IT WORKS

From eyes to insights
in three steps.

The system uses your webcam to detect iris position in real-time, maps gaze to screen coordinates via affine calibration, then classifies movements into research-grade metrics.

01
👁
EYE TRACKING
MediaPipe detects 478 facial landmarks per frame, isolating the iris center in both eyes for sub-pixel gaze estimation.
02
⊕
CALIBRATION
A 9-point affine calibration maps your unique eye movement range to screen coordinates. Keep your head still — move only your eyes.
03
◈
GAZE ANALYSIS
Gaze samples are classified in real-time into fixations, saccades, scan paths, and heatmaps using I-DT and I-VT algorithms.

QUICK START

Up and running
in under a minute.

  1. 01Allow camera access when the browser prompts. Only your face is used — nothing is stored externally.
  2. 02Enter your details — name, age, and gender. These are stored with your session for group comparison.
  3. 03Complete the 9-point calibration. Keep your head completely still and move only your eyes to each dot. Hold until the ring fills.
  4. 04Select an image from the library, or upload your own stimulus image.
  5. 05View the image naturally. Your gaze is recorded automatically. When done, press FINISH SESSION.
  6. 06Explore your data — fixations, saccades, heatmaps, scan path, and first fixation. Compare with others in the COMPARE tab.
