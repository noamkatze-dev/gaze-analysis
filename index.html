HOW IT WORKS

From eyes to insights in three steps.

The system uses your webcam to detect iris position in real-time, maps gaze to screen coordinates via affine calibration, then classifies movements into research-grade metrics.

01
👁
EYE TRACKING
MediaPipe detects 478 facial landmarks per frame, isolating the iris center in both eyes for sub-pixel gaze estimation.
02
⊕
CALIBRATION
A 9-point affine calibration maps your unique eye movement range to screen coordinates. Keep your head still.
03
◈
GAZE ANALYSIS
Gaze samples are classified in real-time into fixations, saccades, scan paths, and heatmaps using I-DT and I-VT algorithms.
