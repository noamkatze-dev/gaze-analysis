DETECTION

How We Track Your Eyes

MediaPipe FaceLandmarker locates 478 facial landmarks in real time via your webcam. Iris keypoints are extracted and mapped through an affine calibration matrix to screen coordinates — all locally, with no data leaving your device.

FIXATIONS

Where Attention Dwells

When gaze remains within a small spatial window for more than 150 ms, a fixation is registered using the I-DT dispersion-threshold algorithm. Circle size encodes dwell time — the longer the fixation, the larger the ring.

SACCADES

The Path Between Fixations

Rapid ballistic eye movements between fixation points are detected by the I-VT velocity-threshold algorithm. Each arrow shows direction and amplitude, revealing how the eye jumps across the scene.

HEATMAP

Intensity of Focus

All gaze samples are accumulated into a Gaussian kernel density field. Marching-squares contour lines then trace iso-density boundaries at six levels, revealing regions of high and low visual attention.

SCAN PATH

The Journey of the Eye

Ordered fixation sequence reveals reading strategy, visual priority, and search patterns. Numbered circles mark each fixation stop; dashed lines show the trajectory between them.

GET STARTED

Your Turn

Follow these steps to begin your eye-tracking session.

  1. 1Allow camera access when prompted — tracking runs entirely in your browser.
  2. 2Follow the 9-point calibration to map your iris to screen coordinates.
  3. 3Upload or select images to view. Your gaze is recorded while you look.
  4. 4Press DONE after each image. Open DATA to explore fixations, saccades, and heatmaps.
