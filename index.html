HOW IT WORKS

From eyes to insights in three steps.

MediaPipe tracks your iris in real-time, maps gaze to screen coordinates via 9-point affine calibration, then classifies eye movements into research-grade metrics.

01
👁
EYE TRACKING
MediaPipe detects 478 facial landmarks per frame, isolating the iris center for sub-pixel gaze estimation.
02
⊕
CALIBRATION
A 9-point affine calibration maps your eye movement range to screen coordinates. Head still, eyes only.
03
◈
GAZE ANALYSIS
Gaze samples are classified in real-time into fixations, saccades, scan paths, and heatmaps.

QUICK START

Up and running in under a minute.

01Allow camera access — your face is processed locally, nothing is sent externally.
02Enter your details — name, age, gender. Stored with your session for comparison.
03Complete 9-point calibration. Head still — move only your eyes to each dot.
04Select an image from the library or upload your own stimulus image.
05View the image naturally. Gaze is recorded automatically. Press FINISH SESSION when done.
06Explore your data — fixations, saccades, heatmaps, scan path, first fixation.
