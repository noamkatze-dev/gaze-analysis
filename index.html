HOW IT WORKS

From eyes to insights
in three steps.

The system uses your webcam to track the position of your iris in real-time, maps gaze to screen coordinates, then classifies eye movements into research-grade metrics.

01
👁
EYE TRACKING
MediaPipe detects 478 facial landmarks per frame, isolating the iris center in both eyes for sub-pixel gaze estimation.
02
⊕
CALIBRATION
A 9-point affine calibration maps your unique eye movement range to screen coordinates. Keep your head still and move only your eyes.
03
◈
GAZE ANALYSIS
Gaze samples are classified in real-time into fixations, saccades, scan paths, and heatmaps using I-DT and I-VT algorithms.

QUICK START

Up and running
in under a minute.

  1. 01Allow camera access when the browser prompts — only your face is used, nothing is recorded or stored externally.
  2. 02Enter your details — name, age, and gender. These are stored with your session data for comparison across participants.
  3. 03Complete the 9-point calibration. Keep your head completely still and move only your eyes to each red dot. Hold each dot until the ring fills.
  4. 04Select an image from the library or upload your own stimulus image.
  5. 05View the image naturally. Your gaze is recorded automatically at 10 samples/second. When done, press FINISH SESSION.
  6. 06Explore your data — click DATA to see fixations, saccades, heatmaps, scan path, and first fixation. Compare across users in the COMPARE tab.
